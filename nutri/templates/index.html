How It Works

A seamless workflow connecting donors, volunteers, and users to combat food waste and support hunger alleviation.

Donors List Surplus

Restaurants, supermarkets, and hotels log fresh surplus meals, shelf-stable goods, or bakery excess before expiry.

Volunteers Mobilize

Local volunteers accept delivery notifications, pick up food packages directly, and perform quick temperature checks.

Users Receive Food

Registered, verified individuals or shelters claim food packages and receive updates once volunteers are en route.
